St. Kateri

St. Kateri Catholic School, situated at 220 Sunnyside Drive in London, Ontario, is a Kindergarten to Grade 8 school within the London District Catholic School Board (LDCSB). There are about 400 students. The school is named after Kateri Tekakwitha, a Native American who converted to Christianity and devoted her life to Christ through prayer and personal sacrifice.

The school offers a comprehensive curriculum that emphasizes academic excellence and character development. Students are encouraged to engage in various activities and programs, both academic and non-academic, to foster a well-rounded education.


For more information about St. Kateri Catholic School, you can visit their official website at kat.ldcsb.ca.
