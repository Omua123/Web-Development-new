About Stray Kids

Stray Kids is a South Korean boy group formed by JYP Entertainment. Known for their unique music, performances, and charismatic members, Stray Kids has captivated hearts all over the world. The group debuted in 2018 and has since garnered a massive following for their hard-hitting tracks, emotional ballads, and passionate fanbase, called Stay.

Stray Kids' music explores a variety of genres, and their lyrics often reflect personal struggles, youth, and self-empowerment, making them relatable to many of their fans. They are truly a global phenomenon!
